<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 12pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in"><span style="background-color: white">As
previously disclosed, on October 22, 2023, Chevron Corporation (&#8220;</span><span style="text-decoration: underline">Chevron</span><span style="background-color: white">&#8221;)
entered into an Agreement and Plan of Merger (the &#8220;</span><span style="text-decoration: underline">Merger Agreement</span><span style="background-color: white">&#8221;)
with Hess Corporation (&#8220;</span><span style="text-decoration: underline">Hess</span><span style="background-color: white">&#8221;) and Yankee Merger Sub Inc., a direct,
wholly owned subsidiary of Chevron (&#8220;</span><span style="text-decoration: underline">Merger Subsidiary</span><span style="background-color: white">&#8221;). The Merger
Agreement provides that, among other things and subject to the terms and conditions of the Merger Agreement, Merger Subsidiary will be
merged with and into Hess, with Hess surviving and continuing as the surviving corporation of the Merger and as a direct, wholly owned
subsidiary of Chevron (such transaction, the &#8220;</span><span style="text-decoration: underline">Merger</span><span style="background-color: white">&#8221;).</span></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 12pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The Merger is conditioned
